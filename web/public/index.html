Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers — for more detail visit the help center.

Is there a free trial?

Every course includes free preview lectures — typically the first two of each section. Browse the curriculum, watch the previews, and only pay for the course if you find it useful.

Do I have to subscribe?

No subscriptions. Each course is a one-time purchase and stays in your library forever — even if you uninstall the app or switch devices. Just use the same Apple ID or Google account.

What devices does iLearnIt support?

iLearnIt runs on iOS 13+ and Android 8+. The same purchase works across all your devices on the same store account. A web player is coming.

Can I download lessons for offline practice?

Yes — sheet music and exercise PDFs are downloadable from the lecture page. Offline video downloads are on the roadmap.

How do I restore purchases on a new device?

Sign in with the same account, open Profile → Restore purchases, and the app re-downloads every course you've ever bought.

Who are the instructors?

Each instructor is a working professional with conservatory training and concert experience. Browse their full bios in the app under Instructors.
