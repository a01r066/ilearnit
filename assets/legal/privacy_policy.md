# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 6, 2026
**Last updated:** June 6, 2026

iLearnIt ("we", "us", "our") operates the iLearnIt mobile application and the
admin web portal (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains
how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you use
the Service.

By using iLearnIt you agree to the practices described here. If you do not
agree, please do not use the Service.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you provide

- **Account information.** When you create an account we collect your email
  address, display name, and (for email/password sign-up) a hashed password
  stored by Firebase Authentication. If you sign in with Google or Apple,
  we receive your name, email, and profile picture from those providers.
- **Profile information.** Optional fields you fill in such as primary
  instrument, skill level, profile photo, and instructor application
  details.
- **User-generated content.** Reviews, ratings, questions, and any content
  you submit through the Service.
- **Payment information.** Purchases and subscriptions are processed
  exclusively by Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see or store your
  payment card details. We do receive a transaction identifier so we can
  associate your purchase with your account.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device and usage data.** Device model, operating system, app version,
  language, time zone, screen size, crash logs, and aggregated usage metrics
  (which screens you view, which lectures you start, how long you watch).
- **Identifiers.** Firebase Installation ID, FCM push token, and (only with
  your permission on iOS) the IDFA.
- **Cookies and similar technologies.** The admin web portal uses
  first-party cookies for session management.

### 1.3 Information from third parties

- **Authentication providers.** When you sign in with Google or Apple, we
  receive the information described in Section 1.1.
- **App stores.** We receive subscription status updates (renewal, refund,
  cancellation) from Apple and Google via server-to-server notifications.

---

## 2. How we use information

We use the information described above to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Create and manage your account, including authentication.
- Process purchases and subscriptions, including verifying receipts to
  prevent fraud.
- Personalize content (recommended courses, popular sections by instrument).
- Send push notifications when you opt in — for course updates, instructor
  application decisions, new content, and broadcast announcements.
- Send transactional emails such as password resets, email verification, and
  receipts.
- Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, security incidents, and
  violations of our Terms of Service.
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for
third-party advertising.

---

## 3. Legal basis (EEA / UK users)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process
your personal information under the following legal bases:

- **Contract.** To deliver the Service you signed up for.
- **Legitimate interests.** To improve the Service, prevent fraud, and
  secure our systems.
- **Consent.** For push notifications, optional analytics, and any future
  feature that requires it.
- **Legal obligation.** To comply with applicable laws.

You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

---

## 4. How we share information

We share information with:

- **Service providers** that help us operate the Service, including
  Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, messaging, hosting),
  Apple App Store and Google Play (payments), and email delivery providers.
- **Instructors.** Reviews and ratings you post on a course are visible to
  the course instructor and to other learners. Your display name and profile
  picture are shown alongside your review.
- **Compliance and safety.** We may disclose information to comply with a
  subpoena, court order, or other legal process; to enforce our Terms; or to
  protect the rights, property, or safety of iLearnIt, our users, or the
  public.
- **Business transfers.** If iLearnIt is involved in a merger, acquisition,
  or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that
  transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to
  a different privacy policy.

We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or
analytics providers for purposes unrelated to operating the Service.

## 5. International transfers

iLearnIt is operated from Vietnam. Information we collect may be transferred
to, stored, and processed in countries other than your own, including the
United States (Google Firebase) and other regions where our service
providers operate. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and equivalent
mechanisms to protect your information in international transfers.

---

## 6. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. When you
delete your account, we permanently delete or anonymize your information
within 30 days, except where retention is required by law, necessary to
resolve disputes, or necessary to enforce our agreements.

Backups containing your information are retained for up to 90 days for
disaster-recovery purposes.

---

## 7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate information.
- Delete your account and associated information (see Section 8).
- Object to or restrict certain processing.
- Receive a copy of your information in a portable format.
- Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email **privacy@ilearnit.info**. We respond within
30 days.

---

## 8. Deleting your account

You can delete your account at any time from
**Profile → Settings → Delete account**.

Deletion permanently removes:

- Your user profile, primary instrument, skill level, photo.
- Your enrollments and progress.
- Your instructor application (if any).
- Reviews and ratings you authored.
- Push notification tokens.
- Files you uploaded to our storage.

Note that:

- Subscriptions must be canceled separately through the App Store or Google
  Play. Deleting your iLearnIt account does not cancel a subscription.
- Anonymized aggregate analytics (for example, "total reviews on course X")
  may persist after deletion.
- Backups may retain a copy of your information for up to 90 days before
  they are overwritten.

## 9. Children

iLearnIt is not directed to children under 13 (or under the equivalent
minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal
information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with
personal information, contact **privacy@ilearnit.info** and we will delete
it.

## 10. Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards
to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at
rest, scoped access controls, audit logging, and least-privilege service
accounts. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot
guarantee absolute security.

## 11. Push notifications

iLearnIt sends push notifications for course updates, new content, and
instructor announcements when you opt in. You can revoke this permission at
any time from your device settings or from
**Profile → Settings → Notifications**.
