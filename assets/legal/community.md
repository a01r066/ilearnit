# Community Guidelines

_Last updated: June 2026_

iLearnIt is a learning community where students, instructors, and staff
meet to share knowledge about music. To keep it safe and useful for
everyone, all reviews, questions, answers, notes, and other
user-generated content must follow these rules.

## What is not allowed

You agree not to post content that:

- Harasses, bullies, threatens, or attacks any person.
- Promotes hate, discrimination, or violence against any group based on
  race, ethnicity, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation,
  disability, age, national origin, or any other protected attribute.
- Contains nudity or sexual content.
- Depicts or glorifies self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders.
- Encourages or instructs others to commit violence or dangerous acts.
- Is spam, scams, phishing, or repetitive promotional content.
- Infringes copyrights, trademarks, or other intellectual property
  rights — including unauthorized arrangements or recordings of works
  you don't own.
- Contains personal information about anyone (yours or someone else's)
  that wasn't meant to be public.
- Is intentionally false or misleading in a way that could harm others.
- Otherwise breaks the law where you or other users live.

## Reporting and blocking

Every review, question, answer, and reply has a **Report** button.
Reports go to our moderation team and are reviewed within 24 hours. You
can also **Block** any user — their content will disappear from your
view and they won't be notified.

## Consequences

When our team confirms a violation, we may hide the content, remove it
permanently, suspend the author's account, or terminate it. We
cooperate with law enforcement when required.

## Appeals

If you believe an action against your account was a mistake, email
support so a different reviewer can take a second look.

## Updates

We may update these guidelines from time to time. Substantial changes
will trigger an in-app re-acceptance prompt before you can keep posting
content.
